About Me

A versatile artist with a keen eye and multifaceted soul, I thrive at the intersection of graphic design, portraiture, and photography. My creative lens is driven by a deep love for animals, the natural world, and the captivating narratives woven through cinema and music. Though I hold a degree in law and currently lend my expertise as a jurist at the Portuguese Forestry and Nature Conservation Institute, my artistic pursuits fuel my spirit and allow me to express the boundless stories hidden within the visible and the invisible. This portfolio showcases the artistic expressions that bloom when my passions coalesce, inviting you to delve into the worlds I envision and translate onto the canvas, both virtual and tangible.
